Who is Cup of Joe?
Cup of Joe is a Filipino pop/alternative band from Baguio.
The band consists of six members

  • Members:
  • Gian Bernardino (vocals)
  • Raphaell Ridao (vocals)
  • Gabriel Fernandez (lead guitar)
  • CJ Fernandez (rhythm guitar)
  • Xen Gareza (keyboard)
